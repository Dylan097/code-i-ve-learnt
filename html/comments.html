Why they're useful

Comments are useful for fixing bugs in your code, if there's something specific that you've found isn't working.

By creating a comment, you can see exactly where you need to fix and find it quickly!

Without a comment in your code, you can spend hours searching for the bit of code you need, so it's best practice to put a comment on each new section in your code.

Since coders work in teams, multiple people will be reading your code. Comments in your code will help the next person find where you left off, or if they see a bug, or there's something they need to change, your comment will help them find it quickly and easily
